RECOMMENDATION
1. Retroactively approve the San Jose State University(SJSU) Flag Raising scheduled
on Monday, August 23, 2021 and the SJSU lighting (August19– August 20, 2021) as
a City Council sponsored Special Event and approvethe expenditure of funds.
2. Retroactively approve and accept donations from variousindividuals, businesses, or
community groups to support the event.
3. Place the item on the August 24, 2021 Council Agenda for action.
BACKGROUND
On February 1, 2005, the City Council adopted ResolutionNo. 72517 which requires
Councilmembers to submit a memorandum to the RulesCommittee regarding proposed City
Council sponsored Special Events and the proposeduse of any funds.
The SJSU Flag Raising was held on Monday, August 23, 2021 in the City Hall Plaza and
sponsored by Councilmember Raul Peralez. This special event was incorporated into SJSU’s
Welcome Week activities to help celebrate the return of SJSU students to campus. The
display of the SJSU Flag on Monday, August 23, 2021, is permitted under Council Policy 2-
1, Section C.3 - Flags Displayed in Conjunction With Official Ceremonial Items: “Other flagsHONORABLE MAYOR AND CITY COUNCIL

may be displayed in conjunction with official actions, ceremonial items, or proclamations of
the City Council.”
To further enhance the celebrations taking place,the City Hall Rotunda was lit in blue and
provided an opportunity for students, faculty andthe community to come together to take
photos and enjoy the display of SJSU pride. City CouncilPolicy 7-14, City Hall Event
Lighting Policy, Sections B.1 and C.1 allows Rotundaand Tower lighting in conjunction with
proclamations for official actions or ceremonial itemsas approved by City Council.
The events were free and open to the public.
ANALYSIS
Approval by the City Council of the event will ensurecompliance with the Council’s prior
direction regarding Council Special Events. In observanceof existing fundraising disclosure
requirements, Councilmember Raul Peralez will reportany cash or in-kind donations
received for the event on their Disclosure of FundraisingReport (DFR-1).  Approval of this
memorandum will enable Council District 3, City departmentsand the Office of the City
Clerk to proceed with the event.  Any cash donations received will be processed pursuant to
the City’s normal financial and budgetary procedures.
PUBLIC OUTREACH/INTEREST
The Office of the City Clerk will post the item onthe City’s Website for the August 18, 2021
Rules Committee Agenda and the August 24, 2021 City Council Agenda.
