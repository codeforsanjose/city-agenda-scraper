A RESOLUTION OF THE COUNCIL OF THE CITY OF SAN 
JOSE  RETROACTIVELY  AUTHORIZING  “FREE  USE” 
FOR ACTIVITIES HELD AT CITY HALL ON SATURDAY, 
AUGUST 14, 2021 FOR A FIRE DEPARTMENT WRITTEN 
EXAM PROMOTIONAL PROCESS 
 
 
WHEREAS, in June 2016, the City adopted Schedule of Fees Resolution No. 77829 for 
the fees and charges for City Hall Facility and Plaza Use; and 
 
WHEREAS, Resolution No. 77829 authorizes City Departments to have “Free Use” of 
the facilities for those events that 1) qualify as official City business; 2) are free and 
open to the public; 3) occur on a Sunday through Friday; and 4) are booked thirty (30) 
days in advance; and  
 
WHEREAS, “Free Use” does not apply to Saturday events, and, therefore, Council 
approval is necessary; and 
 
WHEREAS, the Fire Department, in coordination with the Human Resources 
Department, has requested that “Free Use” be authorized for Saturday, August 14, 
2021 to conduct a Fire Captain recruitment process to establish a new eligibility list for 
future Fire Captain promotions; and 
 
WHEREAS, the City finds that the event qualifies as official City business by providing a 
location large enough to safely and comfortably accommodate the proctors and large 
number of candidates; and 
 
WHEREAS, the City desires to allow for the free use of the City Hall Rotunda for the 
Fire Department written exam on Saturday, August 14, 2021;
