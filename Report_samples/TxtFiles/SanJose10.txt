BACKGROUND 
On February 1, 2005, the City Council adopted Resolution 72517 which requires 
Councilmembers to submit a memorandum to the Rules Committee regarding proposed City 
Council sponsored Special Events and the proposed use of any funds. 
The Brooktree Neighborhood Dumpster Day will be held on Saturday, Aug. 21, 2021 on 
Fallingtree Drive; the Vinci Park Dumpster Day will be held on Sept. 25 on Vinci Park Way; and 
the Flickinger Dumpster Day will be held on Oct. 16 on Ulster Drive. The three events are 
sponsored by Councilmember Cohen. These special events provide residents with an opportunity 
to bring a wide array of items to dispose of safely and conveniently. The events are free and open 
to the public, and will follow all appropriate local public health rules. HONORABLE MAYOR AND CITY COUNCIL

ANALYSIS 
 
Approval by the City Council of the event will ensure compliance with the Council’s prior 
direction regarding Council Special Events. In observance of existing fundraising disclosure 
requirements, Councilmember Cohen will report any cash or in-kind donations received for the 
event on their Disclosure of Fundraising Report (DFR-1).  Approval of this memorandum will 
enable Council District 4, City departments and the Office of the City Clerk to proceed with the 
event.  Any cash donations received will be processed pursuant to the City’s normal financial 
and budgetary procedures.  
 
 
PUBLIC OUTREACH/INTEREST

18, 2021 Rules Committee Agenda and the Tuesday, Aug. 24, 2021 City Council Agenda.
