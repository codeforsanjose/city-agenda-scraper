BACKGROUND 
On February 1, 2005, the City Council adopted Resolution 72517 which requires 
Councilmembers to submit a memorandum to the Rules Committee regarding proposed City 
Council sponsored Special Events and the proposed use of any funds. 
The Great Oaks Movie Night is scheduled for August 30, 2021 at Great Oaks Park on Snow 
Drive & Giusti Drive in San José. This event is sponsored by Councilmember Sergio Jimenez 
and the Parks, Recreation, and Neighborhood Services Department. This special event will 
provide food, family friendly games, activities, health and wellness resources and community 
engagement to the residents of San José. 
Coffee with the Chief will be held on September 18, 2021, at the Southside Community Center 
on 5585 Cottle Rd, San José, CA 95123 and is sponsored by Councilmember Jimenez. This HONORABLE MAYOR AND CITY COUNCIL

ANALYSIS 
 
Approval by the City Council of the event will ensure compliance with the Council’s prior 
direction regarding Council Special Events.  In observance of existing fundraising disclosure 
requirements, Councilmember Jimenez will report any cash or in-kind donations received for the 
event on their Disclosure of Fundraising Report (DFR-1).  Approval of this memorandum will 
enable Council District 2, City departments and the Office of the City Clerk to proceed with the 
event.  Any cash donations received will be processed pursuant to the City’s normal financial 
and budgetary procedures.
